Welcome to urbane

urbane for iOS is a urbaneture store demo app designed by Evan Bacon and written in Swift 2.

urbane was first presented on stage by Bear Douglas and Evan Bacon at the Twitter Flight conference in San Francisco on October 21st, 2015, to show off the power of the Fabric platform.

Discover gorgeous modern urbaneture for every room in your home.

urbane is a sample app created with Fabric, the tools you need to build the best apps.

The goal of urbane is to demonstrate how to build great apps using the Fabric platform, not only with the Fabric Kits provided by Twitter, but also with the tremendously valuable mobile SDKs from our partners including Stripe, Optimizely and Amazon Cognito Sync.
